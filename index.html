    Our Mission

    The talk about caring project has as its primary purpose to promote the empowerment of caregivers in the process of caring for people with dependency, in a home context;

    • The informal caregiver to enable the person being cared for to carry out daily activities;
    • Caregivers their appreciation and that they recognize their qualities and limitations during the care process;
    • Recognition and Mobilization of Personal Resources;
    • Emotional support that helps caregivers overcome emotional instability and isolation;
    • Contact with people who have had similar experiences;
    • The Expression of feelings, emotions and thoughts;

    Services

    • Psychosocial Support

      • Intervention process that promotes people's holistic well-being. in this the support provided by family and friends, caregivers, and is a process that seeks to facilitate resilience in individuals or groups. Socio-emotional learning process in which the individual and/or group acquires skills to recognize and manage emotions. Establish and achieve goals, identify and manage personal and other people's stress, as well as deal with interpersonal situations and/or situations arising from caring constructively. Self-knowledge, resilience, active listening, effective communication, self-esteem and self-confidence, empathy and self-regulation...

    • Sharing group

      • Sharing groups to be held upon prior registration, in which people can share lived experiences in a safe environment and guided by a qualified professional, in order to:- enhance the recognition and understanding of emotions, in an environment where free expression is allowed without judgments. promote empathy and assertive communication through dynamics and the management of emotions.

    • Seminars/Workshops

      • Promote a positive view of Care within the family, training for person-centered care and raise awareness and educate about person-centered care for Caregivers and remaining population. Including training actions in various areas, such as: stress management, geriatrics, care literacy, active aging and quality of life.
