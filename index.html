    Our Mission

    The talk about caring project has as its primary purpose to promote the empowerment of caregivers in the process of caring for people with dependency, in a home context;

    • The informal caregiver to enable the person being cared for to carry out daily activities;
    • Caregivers their appreciation and that they recognize their qualities and limitations during the care process;
    • Recognition and Mobilization of Personal Resources;
    • Emotional support that helps caregivers overcome emotional instability and isolation;
    • Contact with people who have had similar experiences;
    • The Expression of feelings, emotions and thoughts;
